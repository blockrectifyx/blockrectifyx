Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 1, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Blockchain Rectification collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Wallet Information: Public wallet addresses when you connect your wallet to our platform
  • Technical Data: Browser type, IP address, device information, and operating system
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our platform
  • Blockchain Data: Public transaction data related to issue resolution requests
  • Communication Data: Information you provide when contacting our support

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our blockchain rectification services
  • To analyze and diagnose wallet issues
  • To improve and optimize our platform
  • To communicate with you regarding your support requests
  • To ensure platform security and prevent fraud
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

Important: We never store your private keys, seed phrases, or passwords. All issues are been fixed by Our platform AI designed to work with public wallet addresses.

All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols, and we maintain strict access controls to protect your information.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Blockchain data access (Etherscan, BSCScan, etc.)
  • Analytics and performance monitoring
  • Customer support communication

These third parties have their own privacy policies, and we recommend you review them.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities
  • Withdraw consent at any time

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Maintain your session
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze platform usage
  • Improve user experience

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on our platform.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@blockrectifyx.pages.dev
  • Support Portal: Contact Support Page
  • Address: 123 Blockchain Street, Crypto City, CC 10001

11. Important Security Notice

WARNING: Never share your private keys, seed phrases, or passwords with anyone. Our platform will never ask for this information. Always verify wallet addresses and transaction details before approving any transactions.
